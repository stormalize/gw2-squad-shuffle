GW2 Squad Shuffle

This page contains some options for mixing up raids, strikes, or any other activity you're going to run. It also lists some ideas for fashion themes. You may select a team composition and fashion prompt to create an image for advertising your event theme. The in-page preview at the bottom is live content and has a few options for a background. You can generate an image to save or copy by hitting the "Share Image" button.

Team Composition

Standard

  • Normal Run

    "And once more..."

    No build restrictions, but a tried-and-true build you are comfortable on is preferred to focus on encounter mechanics.

  • Build Experimentation

    The label reads: "Danger." It hums pleasantly.

    No build restrictions. Practicing or theorycrafting new builds encouraged!

  • Serious Run

    "I will reduce your mind to ashes before I'm done."

    Bring your big pumper build to demolish everything before it has a chance to fight back.

Eras

  • We Noble Few

    "You seek the heart of Orr? Then you must go to the very beginning."

    Core classes only. No elite specialization allowed!

  • Welcome to the Jungle

    "Everybody form up and do your jobs. It's a long time until daylight."

    Must use an elite specializations from Heart of Thorns.

  • Desert Power

    He took their hope, but now you've returned it.

    Must use an elite specializations from Path of Fire.

  • Canthan Tempo Style

    Honor the empress.

    Must use an elite specializations from End of Dragons.

Weapon Specialist

  • Bonks R Us

    When you absolutely want to turn your foes into jelly, this is your weapon.

    Include a Hammer as a main component in your build.

  • One Kodan Tribe

    Pointy stick hurt.

    Include a Spear as a main component in your build.

  • Archery Regiment

    Our arrows will blot out the sun.

    Include a Longbow or Shortbow as a main component in your build.

  • Student of the Blade

    *Skillfully teleports behind you*

    Include Dual Swords and/or a Greatsword as a main component in your build.

  • A Less Elegant Weapon

    So anyway, I started blasting.

    Include a Rifle and/or Pistols as a main component in your build.

  • Axemaster

    It's not the prettiest weapon in the armory, but it slices beautifully.

    Include an Axe as a main component in your build.

  • Arcane Armory

    A thousand secrets, and half of them are lethal. It's best not to get lost...

    Include a Staff, Scepter, and/or Focus as a main component in your build.

Armor Weights

  • Order of Scholars

    Because of this, we feel we must close Ahdashim to the outside world to protect our magic.

    Light armor professions only.

  • Band of Adventurers

    They're at our door. Pavel is readying the arrows.

    Medium armor professions only.

  • Soldier's Battalion

    Now the war machines can begin their march toward the city of Arah.

    Heavy armor professions only.

Professions

  • You're a Wizard

    The power of the elements are some of the greatest powers in Tyria.

    Must use an Elementalist.

  • A Clone of My Own

    Who's to say we're not all illusions of one person?

    Must use a Mesmer.

  • Fright Night

    Living life on the very edge.

    Must use a Necromancer.

  • Tyrian Scientific Society

    This is a high priority item, as indicated by this memorandum's paper stock and choice of ink.

    Must use an Engineer.

  • Hunting Party

    "My trophies are the best the Great Hunt has ever seen. Way better than yours."

    Must use a Ranger.

  • Heist Crew

    I only lied about being a thief, I don't do that anymore.

    Must use a Thief.

  • Followers of the Holy Fire

    I'm stalwart in my beliefs and immovable in my loyalties.

    Must use a Guardian.

  • Legend Whisperer

    "Tribune? I saw you dive into the Mists."

    Must use a Revenant.

  • Adrenaline Junkie

    Overkill? I don't believe in overkill!

    Must use a Warrior.

Miscellaneous

  • They Keep Ticking

    If all you have is a condi build, everything looks like a condi boss.

    Condition builds only.

  • Guise of the Stars

    "I have a theory that Ascension may be a precursor to the Eternal Alchemy."

    Must use Full Celestial gear.

  • Oops, All Combos!

    I march to the boom of war machines.

    Goal: use as many Combo skills as possible.

  • Heritage

    "Standing united today shows promise that my father only dreamt of.

    Bring at least 1 Racial utility or elite skill.

  • Blinding Tide

    Floor? What floor?

    Bring as many flashy and VFX-heavy skills as possible to blind your team with.
